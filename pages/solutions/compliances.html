Supported Compliance Frameworks

Comprehensive support for major regulatory frameworks and industry standards with automated monitoring, assessment, and reporting capabilities.

SOC 2

Service Organization Control 2 compliance for security, availability, processing integrity, confidentiality, and privacy trust services criteria.

  • Security, Availability, Processing Integrity
  • Confidentiality & Privacy Controls
  • Automated Evidence Collection

ISO 27001

International standard for information security management systems (ISMS) with systematic approach to managing sensitive company information.

  • ISMS Implementation & Maintenance
  • Risk Assessment & Treatment
  • Continual Improvement Monitoring

HIPAA

Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act compliance for protected health information (PHI) security and privacy requirements.

  • PHI Protection & Access Controls
  • Audit Trail & Monitoring
  • Breach Detection & Notification

ISO/IEC 27019

Information security controls for the energy utility industry, specializing in process control and automation systems.

  • Security of Distributed Control Systems (DCS)
  • Protection of Smart Grid Infrastructure

IEC 62443

The global standard for Security for Industrial Automation and Control Systems (IACS) across all layers.

  • Network Segmentation (Zones/Conduits)
  • System-level Security Requirements

NIST SP 800-82

Guide to Industrial Control Systems (ICS) Security, including SCADA, DCS, and PLC configurations.

  • ICS Risk Management Framework
  • OT Patching & Malware Defense

NERC CIP

North American Electric Reliability Corporation Critical Infrastructure Protection for the power grid.

  • Bespoke BES Cyber Asset Protection
  • Physical Security Perimeters

TSA SD Pipeline

Security Directive for Oil & Gas pipelines requiring critical cyber-attack response and recovery.

  • IT/OT Network Segmentation
  • Incident Response Exercises

NIS2 Directive

The European Union's expanded cybersecurity law for critical sectors and digital service providers.

  • Supply Chain Risk Management
  • Mandatory 24h Incident Notification
